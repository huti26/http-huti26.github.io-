How it calculates

Imagine you are trying to fish for a Legendary after hitting Level 7. The Chance to hit a Legendary is 2%. The Chance to hit your desired Legendary is the amount of times that Champion is in the pool divided by the Amount of all Legendaries, which are still in the Pool. There are 7 Legendary units as of right now (02.01.2020). Each Unit is 10 times in the Pool. So the Chance to hit your desired Legendary, assuming 0 Legendaries have been picked so far is:

2% * (10 / 7 * 10) which is about 0,28%.

Therefore the Chance to not hit the desired Legendary is 100% - 0,28% which is 99,72%. Assuming you have 50 Gold, which gives you 25 Rerolls, each Reroll gives you 5 Throws, 125 Throws in total. So the Chance, to not hit your Legendary is (99,72%) to the power of 125, which is about 70%. So you would get your desired Legendary 30% of the time.
